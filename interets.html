Professional Experience

During my tenure as the director of the SoftGraph & Installation startup, I gained substantial experience in the field of design applied to system installation and Office suites. As a key figure in the team, I was responsible for leading and supervising workgroups, ensuring the quality of the services provided. This experience greatly contributed to the development of in-depth skills in relevant areas. My journey with the startup also includes co-founding Softgraph & Installation in 2021, initiated during my training in Computer and Electronic Maintenance at the Institute of Applied Technology. Observing a growing demand for computer maintenance, especially in the installation of computer systems, I took the initiative, with the support of two colleagues, to create a startup specialized in installing operating systems (Linux, Ubuntu, Windows), office suites (Microsoft Office), antivirus, and activating Windows and Office. We also expanded our activities to the design domain. As a co-founder and director, my main role was to guide the startup while overseeing the design aspect, including the design of flyers, Snapchat filters, logos, badges, etc. This enriching experience allowed me to develop a diverse set of skills and actively contribute to the success of our company in the fields of computer maintenance and design.

Operating System

Being an expert in the field of installation, I often took care of installing secure operating systems within my team. Among the systems we deployed were Windows (from Windows XP to Windows 11 Professional), Ubuntu, and various Linux distributions such as Kali and Debian.
Operating systems form the invisible infrastructure of any computer device, orchestrating the operations of computers harmoniously. However, it is essential to have secure and complete operating systems, as any incomplete installation exposes the system to vulnerabilities against attacks.

Microsoft Office

I regularly had to install Microsoft Office or activate it. The installation of Microsoft Office is particularly interesting because the slightest error can result in a failed installation.
Microsoft Office typically consists of Word, Excel, Publisher, Access, and additional versions include Outlook and OneNote.
It (Microsoft Office) is widely used in businesses for word processing with Word, or Excel, which is a powerful spreadsheet application, and includes PowerPoint, the visual presentation creation tool. Outlook is a secure email client, and OneNote is a note-taking application.

Antivirus

L'installation de l'antivirus, cependant, nous utilisons Kaspersky antivirus. Celui-ci possède plusieurs fonctionnalités, notamment la protection contre les virus et les logiciels malveillants, qui est sa principale fonctionnalité. D'un autre côté, il existe d'autres fonctionnalités intéressantes telles que :

  • Firewall : Un pare-feu intégré aide à bloquer les attaques provenant de réseaux non sécurisés
  • Filtrage des spams : La fonction de filtrage des spams aide à bloquer les courriers électroniques indésirables et les spams
  • Protection de la vie privée en ligne : Navigation sécurisée, blocage des traqueurs et protection contre le vol d'identité
  • Contrôle parental : Permet aux familles de surveiller et de restreindre l'accès des enfants à Internet, ainsi que de bloquer certains contenus inappropriés
  • Analyse de vulnérabilité : Identifie les faiblesses potentielles du système et recommande des correctifs pour renforcer la sécurité
  • Gestionnaire de mots de passe : Un gestionnaire de mots de passe sécurisé pour stocker et gérer les identifiants et mots de passe de manière sécurisée
